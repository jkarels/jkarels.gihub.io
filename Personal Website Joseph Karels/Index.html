  • Biography


    Hello, and welcome to my website. My name is Joseph Karels and I am a student at California State University, Monterey Bay studying Business Information Systems. I currently work at Petra Restaurant in Pacific Grove as a part-time server.

    Born in Livermore, CA, my family moved to San Luis Obispo when I was seven years old. I attended Cuesta Community College after high school and received my Associate of Science Degree for Transfer to go to my CSUMB.

    I got married last year in July to my beautiful wife, Aubrey. We enjoy living on the Monterey Peninsula and being a short drive away from our family. I enjoy playing guitar, hiking, listening to music, and different recreational sports.



  • Educational Background


    After high school, I decided to stay in San Luis Obispo and attend Cuesta College to finish my general education classes. I wanted to live in the area for a little longer and save some money by attending a community college rather than having to spend a lot of money and building debt my first year.
    I finished my general education classes at Cuesta College and decided I wanted to attend a university immediately after the Fall semester rather than wait until Summer came around. I chose California State University, Monterey Bay because of its beautiful location, class sizes, and the fact I could get in by the end of January. I am enjoying the university immensely and currently have a 3.75 GPA.
